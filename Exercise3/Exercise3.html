Damian Lillard

    Damian lillard is a 29 year old professional basketball player out of Weber State University. He currently plays in the NBA on the Portland Trailblazers. He was the sixth overall draft in 2012 and has never left Portland since. Some of his accolades include:
  • 5x time NBA all-star
  • 1x all NBA First Team
  • Rookie of the year award
